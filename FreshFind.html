About FreshFind

FreshFind is an online marketplace for buying and selling unique, small-batch food & goods. FreshFind currently support 100+ small-scale artisans & farmers' markets across the Greater Toronto Area. Find everything from homebaked cookies🍪 to craft beers🍺 and small-batch hot sauces🌶️ . Discover real artisans near you and feel good knowing you're supporting them and consuming less processed goods! Everything is made in small batches by the artisans and offered to you directly via our marketplace. (Website https://freshfind.ca/)

Design Process

I focused on redesigning the FreshFind e-commerce website based on my company needs and user testing.
